Admissions – How to Join

Applicants must have a strong background in science and a tolerance for the unknown.

Requirements

  • BS/MS in any field (PhD preferred).
  • Strong record of research or experimentation.
  • Psychological assessment – we’re looking for brave souls.

Application Process

  1. Submit your résumé and a one‑page cover letter describing your most unsettling experiment.
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Attend a virtual interview. Bring a candle.
  4. Receive your acceptance letter – it will be written in a dark font to protect you from reading it.

Good luck, and may the specters guide you.
